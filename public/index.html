PRIEST

The Venerable Dr. Gideon Uzomechina is a spiritual man whose love for the Lord Jesus Christ and his mission to the world has no boundaries. He is an ordained pastor of the Anglican Communion (Anglo-Catholic Church) since 2002 following his seminary training. Being very passionate in his pastoral responsibilities and actively engaged in the community, Gideon brings a message of Christâ€™s love and inclusiveness to his members and to the needy and unreached. About Us

Started in 2018, we bring a new vibrancy to the traditional tenets of the anglican faith. We invite you to join us each Sunday for Bible based study, heartfelt praise, song filled worship, and true Christian fellowship. Our Anglican Youth Ministry provides adolescents aged 5-12 with the resources and mentorship they need to develop a personal, lasting relationship with Jesus Christ.
